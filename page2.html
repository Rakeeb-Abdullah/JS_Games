How to play blackjack

This is a simple version of black jack. We only use 13 cards. All the cards have certain values.Values as following,

  • 1 = 1 points
  • 2 = 2 points
  • 3 = 3 points
  • 4 = 4 points
  • 5 = 5 points
  • 6 = 6 points
  • 7 = 7 points
  • 8 = 8 points
  • 9 = 9 points
  • 10 = 10 points
  • A = either 1 or 10 points (if your score goes above 21 when we get an A card score would be 1 else it would be 11)
  • K, Q, J = 10 points

    • When we click hit acard will be randomly generated and score willbe added. We can hit as many cards as you want. but your score should go over 21.If it goes over 21 You'll be busted.Before your score goes over 21 you should click stand. Then the dealer will play(bot). he will also auto matically put some cards. He will be also busted if he goes over 21.The person to get points nearest to 21 will win.If both players are equal pr both are busted math will be draw. Click deal to play another match.
